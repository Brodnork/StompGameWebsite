Embark on a mission to save the world from the god of chaos in this charming exploration platformer! Explore a world full of lost secrets, make new friends, and use creatures to your advantage to reach new heights in Stomp and the Sword of Miracles!


Life on the island is pretty peaceful. At least, it was, up until about 5 minutes ago, when the Duskman, god of chaos, escaped his imprisonment. Now the whole world is at risk of total annihilation! The Duskman plans to rip the world apart... and to have as much fun as he can while doing so. The only thing capable of stopping him is an ancient weapon known as the Miracle Sword, which seems to have fallen into the hands of a young nubkin named Stomp...


Set off on your journey to save the world, armed with a magical sword and a variety of tools by your side.


Stomp
An impulsive and energetic young nubkin from Roseville who's always dreamed of adventure. After stumbling upon the Miracle Sword, he immediately seizes the opportunity before he has time to think things through.
